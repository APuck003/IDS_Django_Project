Welcome to IDS

History

Founded in 1963, Industrial Disposal was created with the philosophy of total service to the emerging solid waste industry. Today the firm continues to embrace this same philosophy of service, but the scope has expanded to include all facets of the waste industry: Solid, Liquid, Hazardous, Street Sweeping, Sludge and Recycling.
